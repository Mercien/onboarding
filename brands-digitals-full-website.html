About Brands Digitals

Strategy-led. Africa-first.

We're not a traditional digital agency. We're a digital transformation partner — built on academic rigour, practitioner experience, and a genuine belief that African businesses can compete globally.

Our Story

From Abeokuta to Africa.

Brands Digitals Agency was founded in 2017 with a simple conviction: that Nigerian businesses deserved the same quality of digital strategy and execution as their counterparts in London, New York, or Nairobi. Seven years later, that conviction hasn't changed — only our capacity to deliver on it has grown.

What started as a website design and social media management outfit has evolved into a full-spectrum digital transformation agency — serving funded startups, hospitality brands, agro-tech companies, co-working spaces, and professional services firms across Nigeria.

In 2024, our founder Aanu Damilare Oke completed a Master's degree in Digital Marketing & Business Transformation at Rome Business School — bringing academic rigour and global perspective back into our work. That combination of street-level Nigerian market knowledge and world-class strategic education is what makes Brands Digitals genuinely different.

Today, we operate from Lagos and serve clients across Nigeria and internationally, with a selective client roster that allows us to deliver senior-level attention to every engagement we take on.
